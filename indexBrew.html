The Flagship

Spiced Brandy — A Winter Coffee Ritual

Built for evenings, not inboxes. Spiced Brandy is the first full SpiritBrew release to step out of the kitchen and onto shelves.

Expect deep roast, not burnt bitterness. Spice that feels like a slow exhale, not a firework. A brandy note that complements the coffee instead of drowning it.

  • Designed for winter menus, gifting, and “one more drink” moments.
  • Balanced so it can be enjoyed straight from the can or poured over ice.
  • Refined through dozens of micro-batches, real-world testing, and honest feedback.
“It tastes like an Irish coffee and a Christmas market decided to slow down and talk.”

Early tastings have made one thing clear: when people can choose, they gravitate toward the **alcoholic** versions. So Spiced Brandy now leads the SpiritBrew lineup, with future non-alcoholic variants waiting in the wings for a dedicated release.

A Family of Coffee & Spirit Ideas

SpiritBrew started as one bottle on a kitchen counter. It turned into a roadmap. Spiced Brandy is first out of the gate — but it isn’t the only idea in the room.

Below are working concepts and in-development flavours. They’re not all on shelves yet, but they shape where the brand is headed.

Now pouring

Spiced Brandy

Flagship | Seasonal | Alcoholic

Dark-roast coffee, oak-warmed brandy character, cinnamon, clove and soft vanilla. Built to feel like a fireside drink in a can — slow, grown-up, and unapologetically bold.

In development

Gold Whiskey

Future Luxium variant

A smoother, oaky whiskey profile paired with cold brew — less spice, more glow. Designed as a year-round counterpart to Spiced Brandy.

Concept

Black Russian

Short, sharp, espresso-forward

A darker, more direct expression: espresso weight, spirit sharpness, minimal sweetness. Built for the “one more then I’m done” crowd.

Concept

Amaretto

Comfort, almond, dessert-adjacent

Coffee with toasted almond, marzipan warmth and a soft, dessert-friendly character. Gentle, nostalgic, and perfect after food.

Concept

Spiced Rum

Island spice meets night drive

Rum-forward coffee with vanilla, clove and citrus peel. A little brighter, a little wilder, still grounded in proper coffee.

Future line

WhisCoffee (Alcohol-Free)

Non-alcoholic | Development hold

The original alcohol-free Irish coffee-inspired cold brew. Still part of the SpiritBrew DNA, but waiting for its own dedicated release once the alcoholic range is stable.

For Shops, Bars & Cafés

Why Stock SpiritBrew?

SpiritBrew is intentionally small — one founder, one kitchen, one obsessive roadmap. That means early stockists get more than a product line.

  • Distinctive, story-driven drink that actually has something to say on the shelf.
  • Seasonal flagship (Spiced Brandy) that fits gifting, winter menus and “special” fridge space.
  • Direct contact with the founder for feedback, tweaks and custom runs.
  • Early access to AI-powered website tools built under H.A.A.I.L Universe: smart site assistant, future stock-aware widgets and more.

The aim isn’t to flood every shelf. It’s to partner with places where story, selection and taste actually matter.

Built Different

One Founder, A Lot of Coffee, and an AI Lab

SpiritBrew isn’t backed by a marketing department. It’s built by a warehouse worker turned founder, plus a self-built AI ecosystem that helps prototype flavours, branding and future tools.

Every part of this — the drink, the website, the future SpiritGuide assistant, even this static site — is part of a bigger project to show what one person can build with intent, iteration and the right tools.

The Innovation & Intent page goes deeper into the roadmap: AI-powered store assistants for stockists, “What Spirit Brew Are You?” personality quiz, future seasonal lines and more.
