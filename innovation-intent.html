Alcoholic Range — On the Horizon

Gold Whiskey

A rich and warming classic, this Gold Whiskey SpiritBrew delivers a smooth blend of toasted oak, subtle vanilla, and a hint of caramel sweetness. Crafted with real whiskey spirits, it perfectly balances boldness with creamy coffee undertones, creating an indulgent sip that feels like a cozy fireside evening. Ideal for those who want the spirited kick alongside their caffeine fix, this can sets the gold standard for sophisticated RTD coffee cocktails.

Black Russian

The Black Russian SpiritBrew combines the bold intensity of rich coffee with the sharp edge of vodka-inspired warmth. Dark, robust, and unapologetically smooth, this blend delivers a complex dance of roasted espresso and subtle spirit heat, making it a favorite for those who like their RTDs with attitude. A modern classic reimagined in a convenient, ready-to-drink format.

Amaretto

Our Amaretto SpiritBrew marries the creamy depth of coffee with the luscious, nutty sweetness of almond liqueur. With delicate notes of toasted almonds and a whisper of vanilla, this elegant concoction offers a smooth and slightly sweet experience that’s both sophisticated and inviting. Whether savored solo or as a mixer, it’s the perfect treat for those who crave a nuanced flavor profile.

Spiced Rum

Experience tropical warmth with our Spiced Rum SpiritBrew, infused with notes of cinnamon, clove, and vanilla atop a smooth rum base. This lively blend captures the essence of island spice and holiday festivities in one refreshing can. Perfect for beach bonfires or cozy nights in, it’s a spirited twist on your coffee routine that evokes sun, spice, and everything nice.

Spiced Brandy

Celebrate the season with our Spiced Brandy SpiritBrew — a festive fusion of warm cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove layered over smooth brandy notes. This limited-edition Christmas release wraps you in holiday cheer with its rich, velvety texture and subtle hints of vanilla and orange zest. Perfect for winter nights and seasonal gatherings, it’s a comforting cup of yuletide spirit in every sip.
